# Igniting Young Minds Through Coding

At The LEAGUE of Amazing Programmers, we prepare kids for the science
and technology careers of the 21st century through a multi-level program
of weekly classes from beginner to industry certification. We offer
[weekly coding classes](https://www.jointheleague.org/#weekly-programming-classes),
[free Tech Club meetings](https://www.jointheleague.org/#community-programs), and
[many other ways to learn](https://www.jointheleague.org/#more-ways-to-learn).

![Students participating in League programs](https://www.jointheleague.org/_astro/computer-robot.8w74eDDV_Z1rXh7S.webp)

We work to give every student an opportunity for a technical career by running free and low-cost classes all over San Diego County. **We can only reach every student with your help** so please support us by volunteering to run classes or with a donation.
